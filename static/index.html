You are playing 12 Wordle-style levels. Each level has a different word length. Type a word and press ENTER.

Cooldown: you can submit only one guess per 60 seconds.

At the same time, an AI is "solving" the levels too and racing you to the final reward (a seed phrase in the story).

When the AI finishes all 12 levels first, everyone loses.
